Environmental Impact and Cascading Effects

Melting Polar Ice Caps

Rapid thermal expansion and glacial runoff causing unprecedented loss of Albedo effect, creating a positive feedback loop of further warming.

Accelerated Sea-Level Rise

Direct consequences for coastal megacities and low-lying islands, leading to saltwater intrusion into freshwater aquifers and massive human migration.

Loss of Critical Biodiversity

Fragmentation of habitats and shifting climatic zones causing species extinction rates to accelerate, destabilizing entire food webs and ecosystems.
