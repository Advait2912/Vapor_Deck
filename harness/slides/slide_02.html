The Mechanics of Greenhouse Emissions

The Greenhouse Process

1
Solar Radiation: High-energy shortwave light enters the atmosphere.
2
Surface Absorption: The Earth absorbs energy and re-emits it as infrared heat.
3
Atmospheric Trapping: GHGs absorb this heat, preventing it from escaping to space.
4
Thermal Feedback: Trapped heat increases surface and atmospheric temperature.

Primary Forcing Agents

CO₂

Fossil fuel combustion, deforestation, and industrial cement production.

CH₄

Agriculture (livestock), landfill decomposition, and natural gas leaks.

Linear Correlation: Historical data shows a direct, positive correlation between atmospheric CO₂ concentrations and global mean temperature anomalies.
