Mitigation vs. Adaptation Strategies

Mitigation

Addressing the root causes of climate change by reducing the source or enhancing the sinks of greenhouse gases.

  • Rapid transition to renewable energy (Wind, Solar, Geothermal)
  • Reforestation and land restoration
  • Carbon capture and sequestration technologies
  • Energy efficiency improvements in industry
Adaptation

Adjusting ecological, social, and economic systems in response to actual or expected climatic stimuli.

  • Building resilient infrastructure (Sea walls, flood-proof housing)
  • Developing drought-resistant crop varieties
  • Implementing early warning systems for extreme weather
  • Managing water resources for unpredictable rainfall
